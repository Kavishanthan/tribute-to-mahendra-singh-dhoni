Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, also known as MS Dhoni, is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national cricket team. He was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, India. Dhoni is regarded as one of the greatest captains in the history of Indian cricket, having led the team to victory in numerous high-profile tournaments, including the 2007 ICC World Twenty20 and the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup. In addition to his leadership skills, Dhoni is also known for his excellent wicketkeeping and powerful batting abilities. After retiring from international cricket in August 2020, Dhoni continues to play for the Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League.
